Liquid for Gamers

Connect Play, Learn . Africa

Liquid Telecom has the fastest, largest most interconnected network on the continent of Africa. By connecting to homes, ISPs, Mobile Telephone Networks, Global and African Data centres, content delivery networks, we are the infrastructure that makes gaming in Africa possible.
At the very core of our business is our belief that every individual on the continent has the right to be connected. We believe that the power of technology will create better and brighter lives for everyone. ​
Liquid for Gamers brings together the players and communities that we connect and support. ​
Put simply, we connect people.

African Partners in Gaming

Liquid Telecom partners with the best and brightest players in the world of e-sports, video game developers, game and animation communities, gaming cafes. All of these connected over one low latency pan African network, linking data centers in Africa where we host servers for online multiplayer gaming.
